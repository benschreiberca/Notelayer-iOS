# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 24, 2026

NoteLayer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Authentication Information
When you sign in to NoteLayer, we collect the following information based on your chosen authentication method:

- **Email Address**: Collected when you sign in with Google or Apple
- **Name**: Collected when you sign in with Apple (if provided)
- **Phone Number**: Collected when you sign in using phone authentication
- **User ID**: A unique identifier generated by Firebase Authentication

### User-Generated Content
When you use NoteLayer, we collect and store the following content you create:

- **Notes**: Text content and creation timestamps
- **Tasks**: Task titles, categories, priority levels, due dates, completion status, task notes, creation and update timestamps, and display order
- **Categories**: Category names, icons, and color preferences

### Technical Information
- Device information necessary for app functionality
- Authentication tokens for secure access to your account

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Authentication**: To verify your identity and provide secure access to your account
- **Data Synchronization**: To sync your notes, tasks, and categories across your devices through Firebase
- **App Functionality**: To provide core features including creating, editing, organizing, and managing your notes and tasks
- **Data Storage**: To store your content securely in the cloud and locally on your device

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

### Cloud Storage
Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore, which is part of Google Cloud Platform. This includes:
- All notes, tasks, and categories
- Authentication information (managed by Firebase Authentication)

Data stored in Firebase Firestore is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and encrypted at rest. Your data is stored in user-specific collections that are only accessible with your authenticated account.

### Local Storage
NoteLayer also stores data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults with an App Group identifier. This local storage includes:
- Your notes, tasks, and categories for offline access
- Your backend user ID for synchronization

Local data is stored securely within your device's sandboxed app container and is not accessible by other apps.

### Security Measures
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption
- Authentication is handled securely through Firebase Authentication
- Access to your data requires authentication with your account credentials
- We follow industry best practices to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction

These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your information. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand their practices.

## 5. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

### Data Deletion
You can delete individual notes, tasks, or categories at any time through the app. Deleted items are removed from both local storage and cloud storage.

### Account Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app:
1. Open the app and go to **Profile & Settings**
2. Tap **Delete Account**
3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

Alternatively, you can contact us at ben@benschreiber.ca to request account deletion. We will delete your account and all associated data from our systems within 30 days.

Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your notes, tasks, and categories will be permanently deleted.

### Data Export
You can export your data by:
- Accessing your data through the app
- Contacting us at ben@benschreiber.ca to request a copy of your data

### Access and Correction
You can access and modify your notes, tasks, and categories at any time through the app. Authentication information (email, name, phone number) is managed through your authentication provider (Google, Apple, or phone carrier).
